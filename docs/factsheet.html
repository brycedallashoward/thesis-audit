Brand 1: Thesis

Site: takethesis.com  •  Tagline: "Own your mind"

Positioning: "Natural tools proven to support focus, reduce stress and strengthen your mind"

Core idea: Personalized nootropics. A quiz recommends which formula fits your cognitive goals.

What they sell

Four nootropic supplements. Nootropics are compounds intended to enhance cognitive function: focus, memory, mood, mental energy, stress response. Thesis uses pharmaceutical-grade dosing, blind trials, and a data feedback loop from 7.3M customer data points.

Pricing

Each order includes: glass jar + day-tracking lid, 45 days of breathwork/meditation on Open app, Mindshift 30-Day Habit Reset.

Differentiation

  • 9 years of research, 127+ ingredients tested, 30+ beta tests
  • 4x higher dosages than industry standard
  • Third-party tested (microbial, heavy metals, pesticides, active ingredients)
  • 100% vegan, non-GMO, no unnecessary fillers
  • Personalization via quiz (vs. generic stack)
  • Money-back guarantee tied to specific outcomes

Customer Journey

  1. Prospect takes 3-minute quiz on takethesis.com
  2. Algorithm recommends one formula based on goals
  3. Customer subscribes or buys OTP
  4. Ships with glass jar + digital bonuses (breathwork app, habit reset)
  5. Refills every ~25 days
  6. Subscription managed via ReCharge portal
  7. Wellness coach consultations available
Retired products (pre-May 2025 rebrand): Logic, Energy, Creativity, Confidence — the old product names. Still appearing in legacy Klaviyo flows.
