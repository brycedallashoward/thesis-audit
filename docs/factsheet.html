Key Context

What "Hormesis" means in Klaviyo

Biologically: Hormesis is the phenomenon where a low dose of a stressor triggers a beneficial adaptive response. Exercise is the textbook example: small amounts of muscle stress → stronger muscle. It's a real, well-documented concept in biology.

In the account: "Hormesis" was Thesis's internal codename for their May 2025 product relaunch and rebrand — the biggest change in the brand's history.

The Core Challenge

Why the lifecycle problem is hard

The core retention problem is habit formation. Thesis works if you take it consistently. The quiz sets expectations but the product only proves itself over weeks of use. If someone takes it inconsistently or stops, they don't get results — and they churn.

Most of the audience are first-time nootropics users. They're buying hope and a quiz recommendation, not a known outcome. If the first 30 days don't feel dramatically different, canceling is easy. This is a belief and habit problem more than a product problem.

Lifecycle's job
  • Get the customer to actually use the product correctly
  • Convince them it's working before day 20–25 (first refill decision)
  • Remind them to refill at the right moment
  • Keep them from canceling when life gets busy
  • Expand them into a second product
