Done
Review completion rate: 0.88% and declining ~230K eligible over 12mo, only 2,018 reviews. Trending from ~1.1-1.4% (early 2025) down to 0.6-0.7% (early 2026). Benchmark is 1.5-3%.
Done
M2 Okendo Review Request flow: 378K recip, 25% open, 1% click People see the ask but don't follow through. ~20K eligible requests/month producing only ~130-155 reviews.

Ratings & Quality

Done
Avg rating drifting: 4.59 → 4.45 over 12 months 5-star % dropped 82% → 72%. 1-2 star at 7%. Not alarming but consistent downward trend.
Noted
Negative review themes cluster "Doesn't work / expected Adderall-like effects," GI/stomach issues (2+ reviews), subscription/shipping confusion (triple shipments, jar won't open), price complaints, caffeine side effects on Motivation specifically (heart palpitations, agitation).

Product Transition in Reviews

Noted
Product overhaul timeline visible in review data Mar-May 2025: old blends dominant. Jun-Aug 2025: Stress Reset appears. Jan-Mar 2026: Clarity Refill is #1 reviewed product (74 reviews), meaning subscriber refill base is the primary review source. Neuroprotection appearing (5 reviews). Legacy blends at trace levels.

Structural Issues

Investigate
100% of reviews come from "review-request-email" — zero organic/on-site submission No customers are leaving reviews voluntarily on the site. Need to investigate on-site review widget configuration.
Investigate
Nearly zero photo reviews (1 out of 200) UGC is completely untapped. Review incentive structure isn't driving photos. Need to review what incentive is offered and whether photo prompts exist.
Noted
"Thesis" generic reviews: 27 per sample reviewing the brand, not a product Some customers don't know which product they're reviewing.
Investigate
Zaymo integration not visible in review flows Zaymo is actively firing events (3.8K Shown Swap Offer, 2.4K Interactions, 2.2K Delay Subscription in 30 days) but isn't connected to the review experience.
Opportunity: Review request flow needs optimization (better timing, incentive, CTA). New products need dedicated review generation campaigns. Photo/UGC component is completely untapped.

Not started
Campaign strategy is directionally correct. Thesis sends campaigns when they have something to say rather than blasting on a constant cadence. That said, there's probably room for more sends in 2026, particularly to active subscribers who currently live entirely in flows.

Quiz & Conversion CTAs

Investigate
Quiz abandonment logic — is it working? "Leads | Quiz Abandon Research Request" flow exists. Need to evaluate its performance and whether the abandonment trigger is firing correctly.

Creative Performance

Investigate
Audit creative performance across flows and campaigns Open-to-click gap is extreme (36-57% open, <1% click). Subject lines and timing are working but the content inside isn't compelling action. Need to evaluate: email design, CTA placement/copy, content strategy, template consistency, mobile rendering. This is likely the single biggest lever for improving engagement across the board.
Investigate
Compare creative approach across high-performing vs. low-performing flows Refill Reminders (11% click, 23% conv) vs M1 Onboarding (1.3% click) vs Winback (0.9% click). What's different about the creative in the flows that work?
Investigate
SMS creative — is the copy driving action or just notifying? SMS Welcome has strong RPR ($6.23) but promo SMS caused 5.15% unsub. Different creative approaches with very different results.
