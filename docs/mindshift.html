Mindshift: From PDF to Product

Turn a high-click dead-end into a 30-day interactive retention engine

Value-add concept for Thesis + Stasis

The Opportunity

The Mindshift flow already has strong engagement. Subscribers click through to download a 39-page "30-Day Habit Reset" PDF. But the experience ends there. No follow-up. No completion tracking. No behavioral data fed back to Klaviyo. It's a dead-end asset.

Today

  • Email links to static PDF download
  • 39 pages delivered all at once
  • Zero visibility into who reads what
  • No follow-up or re-engagement
  • No data captured for Klaviyo

Proposed

  • Email links to interactive web experience
  • Content dripped across 4 weekly volumes
  • Track which lessons are completed
  • Weekly nudge emails based on progress
  • Engagement data flows into Klaviyo profiles
Why this matters for retention: Subscribers who engage with educational content beyond the product itself are stickier. This gives Thesis a reason to email customers every week for a month with something that isn't a promotion, building habit + brand association during the critical early-subscription window.

Neuroscience 101

Meet the Brain's Messengers: Neurotransmitters

Dopamine is often called the "motivation molecule." It sustains our focus and attention, driving our pursuit of goals. Your willingness to push through a workout or finish a crossword? That's dopamine.

Norepinephrine plays a critical role in our body's response to stress, impacting motivation and alertness. An upcoming deadline triggering a burst of focus? That's norepinephrine at work.

Neuroscience 101

Neuroplasticity, Explained

Just like you can strengthen muscles by hitting the gym, you can strengthen the brain by forming new connections between brain cells.

Neuroplasticity is the brain's ability to change over time, involving the strengthening of synapses through repeated behaviors and experiences.

One of the best ways to boost neurogenesis is to stimulate proteins like Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor (BDNF). Key BDNF-supporting nutrients include omega-3 fatty acids, polyphenols, and Lion's Mane.

Retention

Progressive unlock keeps them coming back

Weeks unlock on a schedule tied to subscription delivery. Week 2 opens when the second shipment arrives. Each return visit is a retention touchpoint that isn't a promo email.

Klaviyo

Progress syncs to profile properties

Every lesson completion fires a Klaviyo event. Profile properties track mindshift_week and mindshift_pct_complete. Lets you segment by engagement depth.

Engagement

Interactive elements replace passive reading

Inline quizzes and reflection prompts turn a static article into a two-way interaction. Quiz responses can flow to Klaviyo as custom properties for personalization.

Content

Product education woven into the science

The neuroplasticity article naturally connects to Lion's Mane in Clarity blend. This is contextual product education, not a sales pitch. Subscribers learn why their supplement works while building brand trust.

Data Signals Back to Klaviyo

Every interaction generates an event or profile property that Thesis can use for segmentation, flow triggers, and personalization.
