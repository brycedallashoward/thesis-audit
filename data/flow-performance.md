## Key Observations

### Revenue Concentration
The Refill Reminders - Hormesis flow (TcGQ3t) accounts for the overwhelming majority of flow revenue. Every other email flow combined totals roughly $700K — Refill alone is $6.4M. The account is running on one flow.

### The Winback Gap
The math is stark: ~5,000 cancellations/month × 12 months = ~60K churned subscribers. The winback flow converted ~96 of them. At $59/month subscription value, recovering even 500 more per year would add $350K+ in incremental revenue. The 48% open rate proves they're reachable. The content and offer need to change.

### Lead → Subscriber Conversion
The SMS Welcome Flow ($6.23 RPR, 5.7% conv) vastly outperforms any email lead flow on a per-recipient basis. The Hightouch email AID flow ($0.02 RPR) is the inverse — massive volume, near-zero per-recipient return. The referral and review flows are in the $2–20 RPR range, suggesting word-of-mouth conversion is the most efficient acquisition mechanism available.

### Devotion List Deliverability
The Devotion Cold Outreach flow has a 5.6% hard bounce rate. This is a deliverability risk that can affect the sender reputation for all flows, including the Refill Reminders flow that is generating $6.4M. This needs to be flagged as an urgent hygiene issue.

### Onboarding Click Rate
M1 Onboarding has a 1.28% click rate over 25 messages to 80K active customers. NYNM Mindshift has a 6.81% click rate over fewer messages to 36K customers. The mindset/education content is driving meaningfully more engagement than the core M1 onboarding. The M1 sequence likely needs a content audit — it may have too many messages, low-relevance content, or weak CTAs.

### New Lead Initiatives (March 2026)
Three new flows were created in the last 3 weeks: Browse Abandonment, Abandoned Checkout, and 50% Off Lead Flow. This signals active investment in top-of-funnel conversion optimization. These are the right gaps to fill (cart recovery and abandoned browse have typically strong ROI in e-commerce). Data will emerge in the next 30–60 days.
