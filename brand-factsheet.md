## Brand 1: Thesis

**Site:** takethesis.com
**Tagline:** "Own your mind"
**Positioning:** "Natural tools proven to support focus, reduce stress and strengthen your mind"
**Core idea:** Personalized nootropics. A quiz recommends which formula fits your cognitive goals.

### What they actually sell

Four nootropic supplements. A nootropic is a compound — plant-derived, synthetic, or a combination — intended to enhance cognitive function: focus, memory, mood, mental energy, stress response. Thesis uses pharmaceutical-grade dosing, blind trials, and a data feedback loop from 7.3M customer data points.

| Product | Promise | Tags | Reviews |
|---------|---------|------|---------|
| **Clarity** | "All focus. No filler." Enhances concentration. | Productivity, Focus | 3,080 reviews, 4.6★ |
| **Motivation** | "All energy. No jitters." Energizes the brain, heightens awareness. | Energy, Productivity | 1,899 reviews, 4.6★ |
| **Stress Reset** | "Level out to level up." Calm and resilient mind. | Confidence, Balance | 573 reviews, 4.6★ |
| **Neuroprotection** | "Sharpen up to keep up." Memory + long-term brain health. | Memory, Learning | 904 reviews, 4.6★ |

**Pricing:**
- Subscription: $59/month first month (currently 50% off promo, sometimes $39), $79/month thereafter
- One-time purchase: $129
- Each order includes: Glass jar + day-tracking lid, 45 days of breathwork/meditation on Open app, Mindshift 30-Day Habit Reset (digital add-ons)

**Differentiation claims:**
- 9 years of research, 127+ ingredients tested, 30+ beta tests
- 4x higher dosages than industry standard
- Third-party tested (microbial, heavy metals, pesticides, active ingredients)
- 100% vegan, non-GMO, no unnecessary fillers
- Personalization via quiz (vs. generic stack)
- Money-back guarantee tied to specific outcomes (e.g., "feel 54% more focused")

### The quiz / customer journey
1. Prospect takes 3-minute quiz on takethesis.com
2. Algorithm recommends one formula based on goals (focus, energy, stress, memory)
3. Customer subscribes or buys OTP
4. Ships with Glass Jar + digital bonuses (breathwork app, habit reset program)
5. Refills every ~25 days
6. Subscription managed via ReCharge portal
7. Wellness coach consultations available

### Retired products (pre-May 2025 rebrand)
Logic, Energy, Creativity, Confidence — these are the old product names. Still appearing in legacy Klaviyo flows.

## Brand 2: Stasis

**Site:** takestasis.com
**Tagline:** "Your brain, balanced"
**Positioning:** Supplement specifically designed for people who take stimulants (Adderall, Ritalin, other ADHD meds or stimulants) — helps offset the downsides.

### What they actually sell

Two products meant to be stacked with stimulant use:

| Product | When to take | What it does |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Stasis Day** | Upon waking, with your stimulant | 23 naturally derived ingredients (nootropics, adaptogens, antioxidants). Protects against neurotoxicity, steadier focus. |
| **Stasis Night** | Before bed | 19 naturally derived ingredients. Reduces crash, promotes sleep, supports mood recovery. |

### Stasis Kids (new product line, late 2025)

- Kids capsule for children on stimulant medication
- Launched ~late 2025, dedicated Klaviyo infrastructure (lists, flows, quiz) in place by early 2026
- Separate kids lead flow, post-purchase flow, backorder flow, waitlist confirmation
- Kids Announcement campaign (Mar 5, 2026): $15.5K from 53K sends ($0.29 RPR)
- Kids Quiz live (Feb 2026)

**Pricing:**
- Subscription: $79/month (Day + Night Set)
- One-time: $119
- "Stasis Night Retention Gift" — being cross-sold/gifted with new Thesis subscription orders
